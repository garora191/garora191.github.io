Pothole Detection Model

Image Classification using Convolutional Neural Network (CNN)

Project Summary
There is continuous degradation of public roadways due to changing weather conditions causing cracking of asphalt and potholes. There is currently no effective way for municipalities to identify and locate potholes throughout the city without tremendous manual efforts.
Using Convolutional Neural Networks, a pothole detection model has been developed for automatically identifying potholes in images of roadways. This model can be used with images of roadways collected from various sources, such as street view cars, dashboard cameras, and/or highway cameras.

Datasets
A total of 665 images of roadways with potholes were obtained from: MakeML.app. This dataset has been supplemented with images from Google Images and DreamsTime for a total of 719 images.
For roadways without potholes, 718 images were sourced from Google Images and DreamsTime.
