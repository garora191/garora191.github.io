Pothole Detection using CNN

The Problem
Reliable road infrastructure is a foundation for socioeconomic growth and a significant public asset. There is continuous degradation of public roadways due to changing weather conditions causing cracking of ashphalt and potholes. There is currently no effective way for municipalities to identify and locate potholes within their roadways

Objective
Develop an image classification model that can detect the presence of potholes in images.

Datasets
A total of 665 images of roadways with potholes were obtained from: MakeML.app
This dataset has been supplemented with images from Google Images and DreamsTime for a total of 719 images.

Methodology
Utilized TensorFlow/Keras to develop a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) model
Include NN architecture and model summary
