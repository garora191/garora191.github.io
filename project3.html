Marketing Mix Modeling for Coca-Cola

Project Summary
As a part of the MMAI program, I worked with Google and Coca-Cola Canada to conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA) on Coca-Cola's sales data to gain insight about the relationship between digital advertising spend and revenue. Furthermore, developed a marketing mix model to recommend optimal advertising mix for different budget levels.

Datasets
We were provided with the campaign and sales data for 2019 and 2020. The campaign data included information on campaign, location, placement, and date, as well as impressions, clicks, views, and media cost. The campaign data was merged with the sales data based on weeks.

Methodology
The campaign data was analyzed at a national level and a city level to identify features that contribute to an increase in impressions and clicks. Conducted a time-series analysis to identify changes in media cost, impressions, and clicks. Also analyzed the sales data to identify any specific campaigns that contributed to increased sales.

For the MMM, I utilized Baysian Regression to model the relationship between marketing attributes to return on investment and increased sales.
