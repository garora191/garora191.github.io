Predicting Consumer Behaviour

Will Joe buy a large popcorn?

Project Summary
Cineplex uses SCENE loyalty program as a strategic asset to collect data on members and use it to gain a better understanding of their customers. While the largest source of revenue for Cineplex is the box office sales, they are looking to improve the sales in the food category. For this project, I utilized supervised machine learning techniques to model the sale of “MenuFood”, which includes food items other than popcorn and candy (nachos, hotdog, etc.).

Datasets
Cineplex has provided a dataset consisting of 170,000 transactions from approximately 7,000 members for 2019 from their SCENE database. The data includes Member ID, Location, Date, Time, information on purchases made, and more.
